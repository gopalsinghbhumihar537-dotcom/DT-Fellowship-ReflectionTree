## Persona 1 — Victor / Contributing / Altrocentric

*Context: Priya, a mid-level product manager. Today she unblocked a junior engineer, ran a retrospective, and stayed late to help a colleague with a slide deck they hadn't asked for help with.*

---

**[START]** Good evening. The day is behind you now. This won't take long — just a few honest questions. No right answers. Only true ones.

*[auto-advance]*

---

**[AXIS I — Locus]**

> **If today were a weather report, what would it be?**
> → **Partly cloudy — mixed, but manageable**

*[routes to A1_Q_HIGH]*

> **You said "Partly cloudy — mixed, but manageable". When something went well today, what made it happen?**
> → **I adapted when things shifted**

*[signal: axis1:internal, routes to A1_Q2_INT]*

> **Think about one decision you made today — even a small one. What were you trying to do?**
> → **Protect someone else from hassle**

*[signal: axis1:internal, routes to A1_R_INT_SOFT]*

**[REFLECTION]**
> "You chose direction today, even quietly. Keeping yourself steady or protecting someone else from friction — those are acts of agency too. The steering doesn't always look dramatic."

---

**[BRIDGE 1→2]** Alright — you've thought about how you handled today. Now let's look at what you gave.

---

**[AXIS II — Orientation]**

> **Think about the interactions you had today. What was the general texture of how you showed up?**
> → **I was mostly giving — energy, help, focus**

*[routes to A2_Q_CONT]*

> **Was there a moment today where you went a little beyond what was asked of you?**
> → **Yes — I helped with something outside my lane**

*[routes to A2_R_CONT_HIGH, signal: axis2:contribution]*

**[REFLECTION]**
> "You moved toward people today — not because you had to, but because you could. That kind of discretionary effort is the invisible glue in any team. It doesn't always get named, but it gets felt."

---

**[BRIDGE 2→3]** Last stretch. You've looked inward — at your agency, at what you gave. Now let's zoom out. Who else was in today's picture?

---

**[AXIS III — Radius]**

> **When you replay today in your head right now, who's in the frame?**
> → **Someone I'm trying to help, serve, or protect**

*[routes to A3_Q_WIDE, signal: axis3:wide]*

> **When you think about the person or group your work ultimately serves — how present were they in how you made decisions today?**
> → **Somewhat — I thought about it at key moments**

*[routes to A3_R_WIDE_MID, signal: axis3:wide]*

**[REFLECTION]**
> "The connection to the person your work ultimately serves doesn't have to be constant — even glimpsing it occasionally changes the texture of effort. See if you can make that glimpse a little more deliberate tomorrow."

---

**[SUMMARY]**

```
Axis I · Locus         →  strong internal agency
Axis II · Orientation  →  contribution — toward giving
Axis III · Radius      →  wide — reaching beyond yourself
```

*Dominant signals: axis1:internal (×2), axis2:contribution (×1), axis3:wide (×2)*
*Summary key: internal_contribution_wide*

> "You moved through today as an agent — you shaped what you could, gave more than you took, and kept a wider group in view. That's a full day."

---

**[END]** Rest well.

## Persona 2 — Victim / Entitled / Self-Centric

*Context: Rajan, a senior analyst. Today his manager gave credit for his work to someone else in a meeting. He felt stuck on a blocked project. He skipped a team standup because he was behind.*

---

**[START]** Good evening. The day is behind you now. This won't take long — just a few honest questions. No right answers. Only true ones.

*[auto-advance]*

---

**[AXIS I — Locus]**

> **If today were a weather report, what would it be?**
> → **Stormy — things kept going wrong**

*[routes to A1_Q_LOW]*

> **You said "Stormy — things kept going wrong". When things got difficult, what was your first instinct?**
> → **Feel like the situation was against me**

*[routes to A1_Q2_EXT, signal: axis1:external]*

> **Was there a moment today where you had more choice than you initially used?**
> → **Not really — it was genuinely constrained**

*[routes to A1_R_EXT_GENTLE, signal: axis1:external]*

**[REFLECTION]**
> "Some days really are constrained. Not every hard thing is a hidden opportunity. But even in tight situations, there's usually one small thing within reach — even if it's just how you held yourself through it."

---

**[BRIDGE 1→2]** Alright — you've thought about how you handled today. Now let's look at what you gave.

---

**[AXIS II — Orientation]**

> **Think about the interactions you had today. What was the general texture of how you showed up?**
> → **I felt like I was owed more than I got**

*[routes to A2_Q_ENT]*

> **When you didn't get the recognition or support you expected today, how did it land?**
> → **I got frustrated and it affected my focus**

*[routes to A2_R_ENT_STUCK, signal: axis2:entitlement]*

**[REFLECTION]**
> "It's worth asking: when we feel overlooked, is it because we weren't seen — or because we were measuring ourselves against what we expected to receive? Both can be true. One of them you can do something about."

---

**[BRIDGE 2→3]** Last stretch. You've looked inward — at your agency, at what you gave. Now let's zoom out. Who else was in today's picture?

---

**[AXIS III — Radius]**

> **When you replay today in your head right now, who's in the frame?**
> → **Mostly me — my tasks, my stress, my wins**

*[routes to A3_Q_SELF, signal: axis3:self]*

> **That's honest. When you think about the most stressful part of today — was anyone else affected by it, beyond you?**
> → **I don't know, I wasn't thinking about others much**

*[routes to A3_R_SELF_HONEST, signal: axis3:self]*

**[REFLECTION]**
> "When we're stressed, the radius of concern narrows. That's not a character flaw — it's how attention works under pressure. The question for tomorrow: is there one person in your orbit whose experience you could make slightly easier?"

---

**[SUMMARY]**

```
Axis I · Locus         →  a tendency to look outward for causes
Axis II · Orientation  →  expectation — tracking what you received
Axis III · Radius      →  inward — your own experience
```

*Dominant signals: axis1:external (×2), axis2:entitlement (×1), axis3:self (×2)*
*Summary key: external_entitlement_self*

> "A day that felt both constrained and unrewarded. Those days are real. If there was one moment where you had a choice — even a tiny one — that's where tomorrow might start."

---

**[END]** Rest well.
